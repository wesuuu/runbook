# Product Guidelines

## Tone and Voice
The AI-Powered Co-Pilot will adopt a supportive and intuitive tone and voice. This approach aims to foster ease of use and efficiency for biotech professionals, making interactions feel natural and helpful. The language used will be clear, encouraging, and designed to guide users seamlessly through their tasks without unnecessary complexity.

## Visual Design
The mobile app interface will adhere to clean, minimalist, and user-friendly visual design principles. Prioritizing ease of navigation, the design will minimize clutter and focus on essential elements, ensuring a low-friction user experience. This is crucial for bench scientists who need to quickly and efficiently interact with the application during experiments. Information will be presented clearly and concisely, supporting intuitive interaction and reducing cognitive load.
